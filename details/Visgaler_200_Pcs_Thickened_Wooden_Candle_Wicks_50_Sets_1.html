Enhance Your Candle Experience with Visgaler Premium Wooden Wicks

Visgaler offers high-quality wooden candle wicks designed for a clean, smokeless burn and a delightful crackling sound. Made from US-grown sappy fruit trees, these natural wicks bring both beauty and functionality to your candle creations. Each wick is carefully thickened to 0.06 inch, ensuring stability and a clear, even flame without the risk of splitting, making them ideal for both professional and hobbyist candle makers.
