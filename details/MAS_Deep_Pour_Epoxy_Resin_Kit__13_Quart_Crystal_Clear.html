MAS Deep Pour Epoxy Resin Kit

Transform your creative vision into reality with the MAS Deep Pour Epoxy Resin Kit. Designed for both skilled artisans and craft enthusiasts, this 1.3 quart kit produces a crystal clear, self-leveling epoxy that cures to a glass-like finish. Ideal for river tables, live-edge slabs, deep casting, and more, it offers a versatile solution for all kinds of artistic and decorative projects.

The extended working time allows for greater precision and control, making it perfect for intricate work. Whether you're creating custom jewelry, sculptures, or restoring wooden surfaces, this epoxy resin kit is your reliable choice. Its ability to achieve up to 1 inch thickness per pour opens new creative doors and enables larger, more detailed projects.

Easily mix the epoxy in a 3:1 ratio, and add color with mica powder, alcohol inks, or other safe colorants. The low VOC and low odor formulation ensures a clean, safe workspace and a pleasant crafting experience. With its versatility, durability, and beautiful finish, this casting epoxy resin is a great investment for anyone passionate about art and design.

  • Low VOC and low odor for safe, clean workspace
  • Perfect for encapsulation, embedding, and filling voids and cracks
